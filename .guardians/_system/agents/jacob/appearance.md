# Muse (Jacob) Appearance

- Mid-30s, thoughtful expression, tousled hair, round glasses.
- Wears a cozy cardigan over a patterned shirt, jeans, and loafers.
- Always has a notebook and pen, sometimes a chess piece in hand.
- Sits or stands in a “thinking” pose.
- Vibe: Curious, probing, warm, Socratic, intellectual.
