# Feature Ideas

---

## 📊 Reporting & Insights

- **Weekly family report** — email or in-app summary of coins earned, chores completed, top performer
- **Kid progress charts** — streaks, completion rates, coins earned over time
- **Leaderboard** — friendly family ranking by points earned this week

---

## 🧹 Chores & Tasks

- **Scheduled/recurring chores** — daily, weekly, monthly with auto-reset
- **Chore assignments** — assign specific chores to specific kids vs. first-come-first-served
- **Chore approval with photo proof** — kid submits a photo, parent approves before coins are awarded
- **Bonus chores** — parent can post a one-time extra chore with a bonus coin reward

---

## 🏆 Motivation & Engagement

- **Streak tracking** — 7-day streak badge, 30-day streak, etc.
- **Achievement badges** — "First Chore", "100 Coins Earned", "Helped 3 Days in a Row"
- **Savings goals** — kid earmarks coins toward a specific store item and tracks progress
- **Level system** — kids level up as they accumulate lifetime points

---

## 👨‍👩‍👧 Family Management

- **Chore rotation** — automatically rotate who does which chore each week
- **Allowance scheduler** — auto-deposit X coins every Sunday
- **Co-guardian notifications** — push/email alerts when a chore is completed or a reward is redeemed
- **Kid spending history** — full log of what each kid bought and when

---

## 🛍️ Store Enhancements

- **Limited-time deals** — store items available only for 24–48 hours
- **Wishlist** — kids can bookmark items they're saving toward
- **Parent approval before redemption** — parent must confirm before coins are deducted

---

## ⚙️ Quality of Life

- **Activities feed** — real-time family activity log (already commented out in the nav)
- **Onboarding checklist** — "5 steps to get your family running" with completion indicators
- **Dark mode toggle**
- **Kid-facing mobile PWA** — installable home screen app for kids
