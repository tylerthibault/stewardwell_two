Cash-Out Rate

How many coins equal $1 of real money?

Default is 10 coins = $1. Change anytime in Settings.

💡 How it works
  • Kids earn coins by completing chores and challenges.
  • They redeem coins in the Kid Store — or cash out for real money.
  • Quick chore (make bed): 5–10 coins = $0.50–$1.
  • Big chore (mow lawn): 50–100 coins = $5–$10.
