Cash-Out Rate

How many coins equal $1 of real money?

Default is 10 coins = $1. You can change this anytime in Settings.

💡 How it works
  • Kids earn coins by completing chores and challenges.
  • They redeem coins in the Kid Store — or cash out for real money.
  • A quick chore (make bed) might earn 5–10 coins = $0.50–$1.
  • A big chore (mow lawn) might earn 50–100 coins = $5–$10.
