Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

Account information: Name, email address, and password when you register.

Family data: Household name, family code, chores, tasks, store items, and child profiles you create.

Usage data: Pages visited, features used, and error logs (via Sentry) to improve the Service.

Payment data: Billing is handled by Stripe. We store only your Stripe customer ID; we never see or store your card details.

Device data: Device identifiers for trusted-device registration (hashed, not linked to personal identity).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Service.
  • To send transactional emails (verification, password reset, billing alerts).
  • To process payments via Stripe.
  • To debug errors and maintain security.

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. Child profiles are created and managed by the parent/guardian. Child profiles contain only a display name, PIN (hashed), and coin balance — no email addresses or contact information. Parents may delete child profiles at any time.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

  • Stripe — payment processing.
  • Brevo (Sendinblue) — transactional email delivery.
  • Sentry — error monitoring (anonymized).

5. Data Retention

We retain account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion by emailing privacy@stewardwell.com. We will delete your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

6. Cookies

We use a session cookie to keep you logged in. We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

7. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Passwords and PINs are hashed using industry-standard algorithms. We regularly review our security practices.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. Contact us at privacy@stewardwell.com to exercise these rights.

9. Changes

We may update this Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email.

10. Contact

Email: privacy@stewardwell.com
